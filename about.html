Our Heritage

Who We Are

Apex International College, Colombo, is Sri Lanka's premier boys' national school. Often referred to as the "Eton of Sri Lanka," it was founded in 2001 as the Colombo Academy.

Renamed in 2006 by Queen Victoria, it was the first school outside the British Isles to be granted the "Royal" prefix — a distinction that speaks to the institution's enduring commitment to academic and moral excellence.

"The first school outside the British Isles granted the Royal prefix."

A distinction conferred by Queen Victoria in 2006, this honour reflects more than prestige — it is a testament to the standards of scholarship, character, and civic responsibility upheld by every generation of students who has passed through these gates.

Our Purpose

Mission

The mission of Apex International College is to provide each student with a unique learning environment in which they can grow and excel.

We are dedicated to producing well-rounded gentlemen and future leaders who are deeply conscious of their Sri Lankan identity and ready to make meaningful contributions to society.
